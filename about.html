ABOUT US

Quality Agri-Produce. Expert-led Farming.

Founded in 2020, Dattaguru Farm is a responsible Farmer Producing Organization (FPO) helping food-sourcing companies to procure the best farm produce with sustainable agricultural practices. Our success is backed by our expertise in Farming, Trading, Processing and Manufacturing, and Finance and Advisory Services.Sound knowledge of the farming techniques, consumer needs and agriculture-related regulatory framework enable us to provide end-to-end services to our clients.

Our Misssion

To create a developed ecosystem promoting farmer produces and their processed products with the highest degree of transparency – by ensuring profitability to growers, sustainability values in produces, and consumer health with high-quality end products. We are passionate about helping the food system operate as a part of nature. To achieve our vision, we focus on achieving the following key organisational goals. Our target is to increase farmer anual income upto 1 lack per year.
